GSC Agency offers comprehensive security solutions for any site, ensuring absolute safety for people.

Our security officers are highly trained professionals with years of experience in safeguarding a wide range of sites. They are committed, responsible, and reliable, ensuring the highest level of protection for your property, staff, and visitors.

We understand that every site has unique security challenges, from preventing unauthorised access to protecting against theft and vandalism. To address these risks, we start by conducting a site survey and risk assessment to define the exact security measures your location requires.

Our management team will develop an effective security plan tailored to your specific needs, while our supervisors carry out regular visits to ensure our high standards are consistently maintained.

Our officers are familiar with site layouts, emergency procedures, and access control protocols, with many holding additional certifications such as fire marshal and first aid training, providing an extra layer of safety.

With GSC Agency, you can trust that your site will remain secure, allowing you to focus on what matters most.

Why do you need Security?

  • Importance of Security

    A robust security programme not only mitigates liabilities, insurance claims, and compensation costs but also reduces social security expenses borne by the business. This ultimately boosts revenue while lowering operational costs.

  • Key security measures

    Ensuring safety on any site involves providing protective clothing, appropriate footwear, regular fire drill practices, and secure facilities such as washrooms. Additionally, secure storage options and access to necessary equipment help ensure that belongings and resources are well-managed and protected.

How we assist you from start to finish

  • Conducting regular patrols to ensure all access points, including windows and doors, are secure.

  • Monitoring and addressing any inappropriate behaviour or noise disturbances, reporting irregularities to site management as needed.

  • Supervising common areas to ensure they remain safe and orderly environments.

  • Managing situations involving alcohol or other potential triggers for anti-social behaviour to prevent disruptions.

  • Identifying and addressing health and safety concerns promptly to maintain a secure environment.

  • Upholding respectful and professional interactions with all individuals, aligning with the values and standards of the site.
